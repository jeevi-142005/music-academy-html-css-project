MUSIC THEORY : Music theory is the language musicians use to explain the structures, formulas, and relationships present in music. We can use music theory to explain why a piece of music has a certain emotional impact on the listener, as well as provide a common system for understanding how musical compositions are constructed. It also allows musicians to communicate musical ideas with each other in the form of written notation.
